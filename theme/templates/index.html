Welcome to the web site of the Apache Infrastructure Team!

The ASF does not have offices or buildings, it's an intangible entity that exists only on the internet. Its only physical existence is the technical infrastructure that enables it to operate, and the team that manages it.

How can we help?

The Apache Infrastructure team exists to ensure the day-to-day operations of the technical aspects of the foundation. If you have questions about or require support regarding email, code repositories, web sites, issue trackers and so forth, we are here to help!
