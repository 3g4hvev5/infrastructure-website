Welcome to the web site of the Apache Infrastructure Team!

A distributed group of indviduals assigned to serve the needs of the Foundation and Projects.

Looking for Help?

The Apache Infrastructure team exists to ensure the day-to-day operations of the technical aspects of the foundation. If you have questions about or require support regarding email, code repositories, web sites, issue trackers and so forth, we are here to help!

Services and Tools

Infra supports a lengthy list of serivces for the ASF, such as GitBox, JIRA, Confluence, Jenkins, Buildbot, etc. We also have a extensive toolbox we use on a daily basis that contains both home brew solutions as well as more "off the shelf" offerings. Feel free to check them out.
